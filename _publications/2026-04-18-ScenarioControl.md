We present a vision-language control mechanism that synthesizes diverse, realistic 3D scenario rollouts — including map structure, reactive agents over time, pedestrians, infrastructure, and ego-view observations. We introduce a cross-global control mechanism that combines cross-attention with a global-context branch, enabling fine-grained control over road layout and traffic conditions for vectorized latent scenario generation.
